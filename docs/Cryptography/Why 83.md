The standard trigram reading producing continuous values from 0 to 82 and none above is the main reason that the standard reading is used. But what significance does 83 have?

Lymm gives a logical reason for why 83 is the most sensible modulus for the eye [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1H3dpTLw8oE5TGQLsjQEk4O9tT8B106N93AH1e5-ag2c/edit).

Other ideas on the significance of 83:
- It is the atomic number of lead, a notable component in alchemy.
